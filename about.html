Tom is your local Gardening Expert

About Tom

Meet Tom, your local representative from Jim's Mowing franchise in Secret Harbour. Tom is new to the area, having just moved his family here from Warrnanbool, Victoria.

Originally from UK, Tom emigrated to Australia in 2018, helping to set up some of Australia's most successfull windfarms. Previously involved in the renewable energy and windfarm industry, he decided to improve his work-life balance. His long-standing aspiration to run his own business led him to partner with Jim's Mowing through their franchise program.

Having greater flexibility in his professional life allows Tom to dedicate his efforts to his work and fulfill his customers' expectations, given Jim's Mowing's reputation as Australia's most well-known mowing brand.

Property maintenance involves various aspects that require attention, and Tom is available to provide a comprehensive range of services to enhance the health and appearance of your grass and plants. He is ready to assist you in turning your ideas into reality and ensuring your desired aesthetic is achieved.

In addition to the usual mowing services, we also provide watering and weeding, pest control, seasonal pruning, mulching and fertilizing, hedge trimming and shaping, garden cleanups (including waste removal), gutter clearing, window cleaning, and any other gardening tasks you may need.

Whatever your landscaping needs may be, our representative is here to offer suitable solutions. Contact us today for a friendly and professional consultation, backed by Jim's Mowing guarantee of excellence.
